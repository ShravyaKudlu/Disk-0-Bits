About Jobly

What is Jobly?

Jobly is an innovative platform designed to help students enhance their employability by providing personalized recommendations based on their resumes. Our application analyzes your existing skills and qualifications, identifies gaps, and suggests relevant courses and resources to help you achieve your career goals.

How It Works

  1. Upload Your Resume: Simply upload your resume in PDF format. Our intelligent algorithm will extract and analyze the content to identify your current skills and qualifications.
  2. Skill Gap Analysis: Jobly compares your skills against industry standards and job requirements. We highlight gaps in the skills you possess.
  3. Course Recommendations: Based on the identified skill gaps, Jobly suggests relevant courses you can take available in your university.
  4. Coursera Courses: To ensure you have access to high-quality learning resources, Jobly also provides recommendations for online courses available on platforms like Coursera.
